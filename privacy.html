Glow Wellness — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Glow Wellness helps you track your cycle, mood, and wellness. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account: email, name, and optional profile photo when you sign up or use Google Sign-In.
  • Health & wellness data you enter: cycle logs, mood, journal entries, chat messages with our AI companion, and community posts you choose to share.
  • Device data: optional notifications, local app preferences, and optional health data from wearables if you connect them.

How we use it

  • To provide the app (sync your data to your account, personalize insights, and power Dr. Najaat educational chat).
  • We do not sell your personal health data.

Where data is stored

Account and synced data are stored on secure servers (MongoDB Atlas). Some preferences stay on your device only.

AI chat

Messages to Dr. Najaat may be processed by third-party AI providers (e.g. OpenAI) when enabled on our servers. Content is used only to generate replies — not for advertising.

Your choices

  • Sign out or delete your account by contacting support (email your developer contact).
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data on your device.

Children

Glow Wellness is not intended for users under 13.

Medical disclaimer

Glow Wellness is for education and self-tracking only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Contact a healthcare professional for medical concerns.

Contact

Questions: use the support email listed on the app store listing for Glow Wellness.
