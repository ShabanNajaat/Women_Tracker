Install Glow Wellness

Free — no app store needed. Add to your home screen in a few taps.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome and go to the Glow link below.
  2. Tap the menu (⋮ three dots, top right).
  3. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Confirm — the Glow icon appears on your home screen.

🍎 iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome).
  2. Go to the Glow link below.
  3. Tap Share (square with arrow).
  4. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add — open Glow from your home screen anytime.
